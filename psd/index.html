Periodic Skill Discovery (PSD)

Jonghae Park, Daesol Cho, Jusuk Lee, Dongseok Shim, Inkyu Jang, H. Jin Kim
Lab for Autonomous Robotics Research (LARR), Seoul National University
Paper (PDF) arXiv Code Poster

PSD learns periodic behaviors by mapping states onto a circular latent space.

Abstract

We propose Periodic Skill Discovery (PSD), an unsupervised objective for discovering periodic behaviors in reinforcement learning. PSD trains a family of encoders \(\{\phi_L\}\) that embed states onto a circle so that temporal progression corresponds to angular motion, enabling multi-timescale periodicity. The objective encourages state displacement at period \(L\) while regularizing antipodal collapse, together with Lipschitz-style constraints for local steps. PSD yields interpretable circular latents and discovers robust periodic skills across diverse continuous-control tasks.
