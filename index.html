Hi, I'm a Ph.D. student at Seoul National University, advised by H. Jin Kim. My research explores how robots autonomously learn behavioral skills via reinforcement learning. My current interests include unsupervised reinforcement learning and leveraging temporal structure in RL.
Outside of research, I enjoy reading, especially works by Milan Kundera and Albert Camus.
